SMALL BUSINESS CHALLENGES:
- Limited IT budget and staff
- Lack of technical expertise
- Time constraints
- Overwhelming technical jargon
- Unclear ROI on security investments
- Compliance confusion

COMMON THREATS (prioritised by frequency):
1. Phishing attacks (90% of breaches start here)
2. Ransomware
3. Business email compromise
4. Weak passwords
5. Unpatched software
6. Unsecured Wi-Fi
7. Social engineering
8. Insider threats

INDUSTRY-SPECIFIC RISKS:
- Retail: POS malware, customer data theft
- Healthcare: Patient data breaches
- Professional services: Client confidentiality
- Manufacturing: Industrial espionage
- Hospitality: Guest data, payment processing
