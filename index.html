Why They're Targeted

Small businesses are seen as easy targets by cybercriminals.

They hold valuable customer data, connect to larger supply chains, and often have limited security — making them high-reward, low-effort victims.

Common attacks: phishing, ransomware, data theft, and business email compromise.

Real World Blockers

Most security frameworks assume you have dedicated IT teams and plenty of time, but most small businesses don’t.

I researched common blockers:

  • Too busy running day to day operations
  • Tight budgets limit access to expert help
  • Overwhelming technical jargon in advice
  • No clear, simple starting point for action

Why Advice Often Fails

Frameworks like NIST and ISO 27001 are powerful, but designed for large organsations with big teams and budgets.

For small business owners, they often feel confusing, time-consuming, and out of reach.

This leaves many without the guidance they need to protect themselves effectively.
