Understanding Cyber Risk

Why This Matters

Small businesses get attacked 43% more than big companies. Criminals know you have valuable data but fewer defenses.

Understanding risk helps you make smart security decisions.

The Risk Formula

Risk = Threat × Vulnerability × Impact

This simple formula helps you evaluate any security decision. High risk areas need immediate attention, while low risk areas can wait.

Threat: What could attack you (hackers, malware, etc.)
Vulnerability: Your weak spots (old software, weak passwords)
Impact: How much damage it would cause
